Guide to Treaties

This guide is intended to provide an overview on locating and citing treaties.

The United States publishes official copies of bilateral and multilateral treaties to which the United States is a party. The United States Department of State has published several treaty series over the years. Additionally, due to the Senate’s advice and consent role, see U.S. Const. art. II, § 2, the Legislative Branch also publishes United States Treaties.

Executive Branch Treaty Sources

  • United States Treaty Series, 1795–1949 (Bevens)
  • United States Treaties and Other International Agreements, 1950–Present (U.S.T.)
